The browser, Safari

Written by: Nathan van de Kolk, Joran van de Kolk, Do Toan Nguyen

History

Apple & iOS

Safari web browser is the default for the iPhone, iPad, and macOS. The first version was created by Apple in 2003 and could also be downloaded on Windows from 2007 to 2012. Safari is pretty much a exclusive browser of Apple. That is the reason why it is not compatibley these days with Windows. The popularity of the Safari browser is depended on iPhone and the iPad. Since Apple products are pretty popular it automatically made Safari popular and have a high usage[1].
